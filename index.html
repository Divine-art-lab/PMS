About Us

P.M.S was Founded in 2015 by Mr. Michael Olorunfemi, Point de Repère was established with a vision to nurture the next generation of leaders. Guided by the motto, “Nurturing Excellent Minds”, the school is dedicated to providing quality education, innovative learning, and fostering personal growth. Mr. Olorunfemi’s passion for excellence and holistic development drives the institution to shape minds that are prepared to make a positive impact in society.
